The Principle

Down to Cipher?

Down to Cipher is a simple application that provides a counter and functions to set and reduce it to/by different amounts.

On any average day you will find yourself doing repetitive tasks. Often it is the case that you need to do the task for a limited number of times. So, to aid you in that endeavour we present to you Down to Cipher. You can set, reset and the reduce the counter that shows up on the application page and with it track activities that you are doing. To make that even more easier, you can also set the title of the page to the activity that the counter is supposed to track.

The Developer

Why build Down to Cipher?

Down to Cipher struck me as a need when I was working on my daily routine tasks. Now, since most of my time is spent with devices that stay online, I decided that I needed a solution that works on web and can provide me what I needed which is essentially a counter, which also tracks history of edits and comes in a nice beautiful visual package. So, Down to Cipher was built.
